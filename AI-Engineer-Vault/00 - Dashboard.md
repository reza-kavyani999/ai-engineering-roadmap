## How to use this vault

1. Open a phase note, work through its checklist item by item.
2. When you finish something, check it off *and* jot a one-line note under that phase's "Notes" section — future-you will thank present-you.
3. When every item in a phase is checked, check the phase off here on the dashboard, then move to the next one.
4. Use the [[Learning Log]] for anything that doesn't fit neatly into a single phase — debugging sessions, side questions, things you want to revisit.

Don't split focus across phases. One at a time, same as the plan.
